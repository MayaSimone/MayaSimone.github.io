Rocky: Stabilization of an Wheeled Inverted Pendulum

For the final project of our Engineering Systems Analysis class, we were tasked with designing and implementing a control system to maintain the balance of an inverted pendulum when presented with angular displacement. The 'Rocky' bot was comprised of a Pololu Balboa 32U4 robot kit and a 200g mass on a pair of 0.6m laser-cut MDF side extensions, raising the center of mass to create the inverted pendulum.
